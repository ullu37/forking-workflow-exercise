# Pasta boscaiola recipe


## Ingredients

- rigatoni
- pancetta
- peas
- mushrooms
- onions
- tuna


## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Finely chop the onion, slice the mushrooms, and dice the pancetta.
3. In a large pan over medium heat, cook the pancetta until it starts to brown and release its fat.
4. Add the onion to the pan and cook until soft and translucent.
5. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and begin to brown.
6. Stir in the peas and cook for 2 to 3 minutes until heated through.
7. Meanwhile, cook the rigatoni in boiling water until al dente.
8. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
9. Add the rigatoni to the pan and toss everything together.
10. Add a splash of reserved pasta water as needed to bind the sauce and coat the pasta.
11. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
12. Serve hot.
